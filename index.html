The Three Elements

What are The Three Elements?

  1. The Audience. Who are you presenting to? What do they care about?
  2. The Story. What's the narrative you want to share?
  3. The Delivery. How will you share your ideas?

The three elements work together.

Overlook any one, and your presentation won't be effective.

The Story

What's your narrative?

  • The new system will generate 20% more leads.
  • Pre-planning would've let us deliver a month earlier.
  • This new workflow will reduce the chance of errors.

What's the depth of understanding?

Different people have different depths of understanding.

You already know your audience. What's their depth? What do they really need to know?

Don't go too deep, else your audience may drown!

Effective Practice Habits

Practice doesn't make perfect. Perfect practice is what you need to practice.

  • Speak aloud!
  • Practice your posture.
  • Practice like it's real (with a live audience when you can).

Good practice focuses more on replicating the conditions than memorizing material.
